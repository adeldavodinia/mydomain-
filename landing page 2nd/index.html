Mazafati dates, known as the "King of Dates," are prized for their soft texture and rich caramel-like sweetness. Grown in Iran's Kerman province, they offer natural energy and essential nutrients.

Sayer dates, also known as "Starbox dates," are semi-dry with a rich, caramel-like taste. Grown in Iran's Khuzestan province, they are high in fiber and energy, making them a nutritious and delicious snack.

Zahedi dates, known for their golden color and firm texture, offer a delightful balance of natural sweetness and mild nutty flavor. Grown primarily in Iran’s Fars and Kerman provinces.

Barhi dates are incredibly soft, sweet, and melt instantly in your mouth. Known for their rich, honey-like flavor and creamy texture, they make a delicious and energizing natural treat. Perfect for a quick.

Kabkab dates are delightfully soft and juicy with a deep, caramel-like sweetness. Known for their smooth texture and rich flavor, they make a perfect natural energy booster. A truly delicious and nutritious choice!

Medjool dates, often called the "king of dates," are prized for their large size, ultra-soft texture, and rich caramel flavor. Naturally sweet and packed with nutrients, they make a perfect energy-boosting snack or natural dessert.

Gentar dates are cherished for their firm yet tender texture and balanced sweetness with a hint of buttery richness. Grown in Iran's date-growing regions, these nutritious gems provide sustained energy while satisfying cravings naturally.

Piarom dates, known as "chocolate dates," boast a semi-dry texture with a rich, caramel-like sweetness. Grown in Iran's Hormozgan province, these premium dates offer natural energy and essential nutrients in every bite.
